How the prototype handles offline intake
If the network is unavailable, the form stores your report locally in the browser queue and gives you a temporary queue ID.
As soon as the page comes back online, the queue tries to post each report to Supabase and replaces the local draft with a real tracking ID.
This is a prototype convenience layer, not a full audited offline sync engine yet.
